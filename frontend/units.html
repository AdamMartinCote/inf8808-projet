Comparison of the distribution of units between elite players

This visualisation compares the unit composition of two opposing pro players. Each line represent the lifespan of a single unit. The thickness of the lines represents the supply used by each unit (a probe occupies one supply and an immortal occupies 4). Each player can only have a total of 200 supplie's worth of units.

The bottom graph shows aggregated unit supply of active units in each category as time progresses throughout the game.
