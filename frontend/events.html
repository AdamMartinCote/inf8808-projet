Comparison of the actions of two elite players

This visualisation presents the events from the actions made by both players. The events are separated in 3 categories:

1. Camera (scrolling, clicking on the minimap, pressing location hotkeys...)
2. Selection (selcting units and buildings, pressing control group hotkeys...)
3. Commands (casting a unit ability, telling a unit to move, to attack, to build something...)
The maps below show the location of the actions. You can also select a time window from the graphs at the bottom by clicking on them. The points on the map will update to show only the ones made during that time!
